## ğŸ”§ Workflow

1. **Initial Setup:**
   - Copy `.env.example` to `.env`
   - `php artisan key:generate`
   - `php artisan migrate:fresh --seed`

2. **Create Module:**
   - `chmod +x ./scripts/generate-module.sh`
   - `./scripts/generate-module.sh ModuleName`
   - `php artisan migrate:fresh --seed`

3. **Backup & Restore:**
   - `./scripts/backup-db.sh`
   - Make changes
   - `./scripts/restore-db.sh` (if needed)
